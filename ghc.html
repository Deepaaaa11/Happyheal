Health Plans

Whole Body Check Up

  • Over night fasting is required
  • Done once in a year or as advised by the doctor
  • Test:
    1. Blood Profile
    2. Infection screening
    3. Liver Health
    4. Kidney Health
    5. Diabetes Profile
    6. Heart Health
    7. Thyroid Health
    8. Cancer Screening
    9. USG
    10. Lung Health
    11. Doctor Consultation

Post Covid Check Up

  • Recommended for patient who tested negative in PCR but still showing major post covid symptoms
  • Test:
    1. Blood Profile
    2. Infection screening
    3. Liver Health
